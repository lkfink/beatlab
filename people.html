Who we are

Principal Investigator:
Prof. Dr. Lauren Fink

I'm an asst. Professor in the Dept. of Psychology, Neuroscience & Behaviour, a member of the McMaster Insitute for Music & the Mind, and an affiliate of the School of Computational Science & Engineering. Prior to coming to McMaster and starting the BEAT Lab, I lived in Germany, California, England, and Ohio. My training as an orchestral percussionist with a focus on "experimental music" informs many of my research interests. I'm grateful to have the opportunity to work with incredible students and collaborators at McMaster and across the globe!

PhD Student:
Shreshth Saxena

I'm interested in scalable methods of eye-tracking and their application to study audiovisual attention in dynamic real-world, music listening contexts. Prior to my Ph.D., I graduated with a Bachelors and Masters in Computer Science from University of Delhi, India, and gained valuable experience working as a Computer Vision developer in the industry. I'm always excited to discuss accessible technologies, mathematical/programming challenges, retro games and guitar riffs!
